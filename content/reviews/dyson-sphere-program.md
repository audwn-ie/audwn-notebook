## First Impressions

Dyson Sphere Program drops you onto a lonely planet with nothing but a mech suit and a staggering goal: harness the energy of an entire star.

## Verdict

An essential entry in the factory genre. The Dark Fog expansion adds a meaningful combat threat without compromising the builder's soul.
